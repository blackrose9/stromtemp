Mission Statement

Strom Control Systems (StCS) will seek to be the leading provider of quality and value electrical products in the low and medium voltage industry in Zambia by making available globally acknowledged and leading edge products

Vision

To establish the company as a reliable supplier of quality and good value electrical components and equipment. This will be achieved by gaining a careful understanding of local application, regulations and requirements, by working closely with the regular users of our equipment and end users. We perceive ourselves as an organisation that will make a contribution towards the safe use of electrical products by ensuring that the highest standards of selection, safety and reliability are available to the majority of users in Zambia. We will seek to establish mutually beneficial relationships with all our customers to meet this goal.

Market Approach

Strom Control Systems Ltd will seek to meet the requirements of the electrical industry in Zambia by focusing on availing globally accepted practice in the domestic and low to medium voltage industry in the country. Our passion is to avail to the market good value electrical equipment with the best of application safety features

The company will continually seek to maintain high levels of integrity in its dealings with all its stakeholders. As responsible corporate citizen, we will ensure that our staff, customers, suppliers and statutory bodies perceive us as a reliable and trustworthy partner in our dealings
